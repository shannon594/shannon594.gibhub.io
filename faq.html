FAQ


Here are some of the frequently questions our core staff members have answered:


1. How many pieces in each genre can I submit?

Fiction: 1 Nonfiction: 1 Poetry: 5 Art: 3; No previouslypublished work, please.

2. What is the word count that I need to follow?

Fiction and nonfiction limit is 3,500 words delivered in Microsoft Word. No limit on poetry, though poems of over two pages are discouraged. Submit art as jpg file. No pdfs, please.

3. Do I need to submit a cover page in a separate file apart from my work?

Your work is a blind submission. Our submission form has your name and other information. Please do not put your name on your work, or in a header.


4. Do I need to submit all pieces at once, or can I continue to submit work of different genres throughout the submission period?

As long as you do not submit more than what the section allows, you can return to the submissions page and contribute more work(s) until the deadline for submissions has passed. For example, you could submit your fiction story one week and then return the next to submit a couple poems.
