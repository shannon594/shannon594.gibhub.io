About Inscape

Every year, Inscape designates the best Fiction, Nonfiction, Poetry and Art to receive our Editors Choice Award. To check out the winning works, head over to the Washburn University Book Store where you can buy the issues from 2009 and forward. For those of you interested in submitting Art or Writing, please view the submissions page for guidelines as well as the FAQ page for commonly asked questions about the submission process.


Purchase

Current and previous editions of Inscape are available through the Icabod Shop. They can be purchased on campus or via their website.

2017 Issue

The newest issue of Inscape was released in April 2017 and features a diverse group or writers and artists from across the world.

Submit

Inscape accepts submissions during designated periods throughout the year. Please contact inscape@washburn.edu with any questions about sumbissions.
